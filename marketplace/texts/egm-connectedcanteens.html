The product offers a comprehensive solution to address the significant issue of food waste in school canteens, where between 30 and 40% of food is typically discarded. By integrating weight sensors beneath waste bins, the system provides real-time data on the amount of food waste generated daily. This information is accessible through a web platform or a mobile application, allowing schools to monitor and analyze waste trends over time.

One of the primary use cases of this product is the enhancement of menu quality. By understanding which meals result in the most waste, schools can refine their meal proposals and adjust portion sizes to better match student preferences and consumption patterns. This data-driven approach helps in reducing waste by ensuring that meals are both appealing and appropriately portioned.

Another significant use case is the gamification aspect, which encourages competition among schools within a town to minimize waste. This friendly competition not only motivates schools to adopt more sustainable practices but also fosters a community-wide commitment to reducing food waste.

The product also supports environmental sustainability by providing the option to use collected bio-waste for composting. This not only reduces the amount of waste sent to landfills but also promotes the recycling of organic materials into valuable compost, which can be used to enrich school gardens or local community projects.

Furthermore, the system serves as an educational tool, raising awareness among pupils about the impact of food waste. By presenting concrete data, schools can engage students in discussions and activities focused on sustainability and responsible consumption, fostering a culture of environmental consciousness from a young age.

Overall, this product offers a multifaceted approach to reducing food waste in school canteens, combining technology, education, and community engagement to drive meaningful change.
