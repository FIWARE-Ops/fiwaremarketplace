WiseTown is a versatile middleware solution designed to streamline data processing and analysis for city operators, providing them with actionable insights to address specific urban issues efficiently. This open-source, cloud-based infrastructure leverages advanced FIWARE technologies to support cities in their transition to smart cities, aligning with innovative European standards and enhancing safety, inclusivity, and sustainability.

The platform comprises several customizable modules tailored to meet the diverse needs of smart city management:

  • WiseTown Geoanalytics: This Decision Support System aids city administrators and public administration professionals by offering maps, graphs, infographics, and statistics. It distills complex data into valuable insights, facilitating informed urban planning decisions.
  • WiseTown Issue Manager: This digital system empowers citizens to report city issues, thereby supporting city governance. It also streamlines public administration workflows by automating processes, enhancing operational efficiency.
  • WiseTown Open Data Manager: This module aggregates data based on customizable criteria to create Open Data catalogs for smart cities. It ensures that public administration data is treated as a common good, maintaining anonymity and GDPR compliance.
  • WiseTown Situation Room: Designed for managing urban events and emergencies, this platform collects city-generated data and presents it via a dashboard for immediate analysis and response.
  • WiseTown Crowd Planning: This platform facilitates communication between public administrations and citizens regarding planning objectives, fostering active citizen involvement in participatory urban planning.
  • WiseTown Thematic Dashboard: This application enables the creation of custom visualizations to compare map information with statistical and real-time satellite data. It can manage a wide range of data, including economic, demographic, social, and environmental metrics.
