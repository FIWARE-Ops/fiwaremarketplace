Netzlink, a prominent IT and managed service provider in the European FIWARE community, offers a range of use cases for its OpenStack and open data services. As the German provider of the FIWARE environment, Netzlink supports various sectors by leveraging its expertise in IT systems, applications, and data management, all hosted in German data centers. With ISO certification and adherence to the German Federal Data Protection Act (BDSG) and ITIL practices, Netzlink ensures secure and efficient data handling.

One significant use case is the support for EU-funded start-up companies through the first German FIWARE node, established by Netzlink in 2016. This initiative has expanded to serve a broader customer base, including sectors such as health, energy, and agriculture. Towns, cities, communities, and developers also benefit from the FIWARE platform, which provides all Generic Enablers necessary for application development and deployment.

Netzlink's FIWARE platform has facilitated the implementation of over 100 applications worldwide, serving as a robust foundation for innovation. These applications demonstrate the platform's versatility and capability to address diverse industry needs. For instance, in the health sector, FIWARE can be used to develop applications that enhance patient care and streamline hospital operations. In the energy sector, it supports the creation of smart grid solutions that optimize energy distribution and consumption.

In agriculture, FIWARE enables the development of smart farming applications that improve crop management and resource efficiency. Additionally, urban areas can leverage FIWARE to create smart city solutions that enhance public services and infrastructure management. Netzlink's comprehensive support, including its Helplink IT service offering 24/7 professional assistance, ensures that clients can effectively utilize these technologies to drive innovation and efficiency in their respective fields.
