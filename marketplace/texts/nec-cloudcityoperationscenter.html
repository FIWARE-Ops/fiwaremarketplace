The NEC Cloud City Operations Centre (CCOC) platform serves as the central hub for smart city management, leveraging big data from various sources like historical databases, IoT sensors, and CCTV to transform city services. The City Operations Centre Predictive Analytics is a managed service that helps cities interpret and automate responses to collected data. This service includes several CCOC modules that clients can use to create data visualizations tailored to specific use cases.

The main modules of the CCOC platform include:

  • Dashboard (Content Management System): Configurable with widgets to display a customizable subset of data in various formats, including alarm generation capabilities.
  • Big Data Storage: Manages and processes large data volumes from IoT devices, providing real-time analyses and historical data aggregation.
  • IoT Broker: Connects to any IoT device without protocol or communication constraints.
  • ESB (Enterprise Service Bus): Integrates with legacy systems and applications using existing APIs and interfaces.
  • Streaming Media Server: Manages video streaming on the platform.
  • Real-Time Processing Engine: Processes events online for immediate action.
  • API: Exposes data to upper layers for further utilization.
  • Publish/Subscription Notification Engine: Allows systems to subscribe to specific information.
  • BPM & BRE (Rules and Workflows Engines): Automates manual processes through rules and workflows.
  • Analytics and Prediction Engine: Uses algorithms to analyze past events, predict future occurrences, and prescribe actions.
  • BI (Business Intelligence): Generates KPI reports for city analytics, exportable in multiple formats like HTML, PDF, and Excel.

All tools and data are accessible via a consolidated, user-friendly web-based interface. The NEC CCOC platform is developed according to industry standards, such as FIWARE, facilitating easy integration with other or third-party systems.
