The WiseTown Thematic Dashboard offers a versatile platform for urban planners, city officials, and researchers to gain insights into various urban phenomena through customized views that integrate cartographic information with statistical and real-time satellite data. This application is particularly beneficial for those who require a high degree of configurability in their data analysis tools.

One of the primary use cases is the creation of time-lapse satellite dashboards, which allow users to observe and compare the evolution of specific phenomena over time. This feature is invaluable for tracking changes in urban development, environmental conditions, and other dynamic factors affecting cities.

Another significant application is in the analysis of public parks and gardens. The dashboards can generate reports on the health of plants and vegetation, providing essential data for urban forestry management and environmental conservation efforts.

The platform also supports dashboards that assess the degree of urban land consumption, offering insights into how land is being utilized and the implications for sustainable development. This is crucial for city planners and policymakers aiming to balance growth with environmental preservation.

Social and economic data dashboards provide a comprehensive view of population demographics, enabling targeted policy-making and resource allocation. These dashboards can include quality of life indicators, offering a holistic view of urban living conditions.

Mobility flow analysis dashboards are another key feature, helping to understand traffic patterns and optimize transportation systems. This can lead to improved urban mobility and reduced congestion.

Finally, the platform offers dashboards for monitoring city and territorial safety, providing real-time data that can be used to enhance public safety measures and emergency response strategies.

Overall, the WiseTown Thematic Dashboard is a powerful tool for anyone involved in urban management and planning, offering interactive, map-based dashboards with customizable filters and selections to tailor the display of information to specific needs.
