CuraVestaÂ© is a mobile application designed to support individuals who care for their relatives, enabling them to live independently and safely at home. This innovative mHealth service is accessible to both caregivers and those in need of care, providing assistance anytime and anywhere. The system comprises three main components that facilitate home care:

  • Nursing Guide: This component helps caregivers accomplish daily tasks and alleviates the mental stress associated with caregiving. It provides knowledge on executing typical tasks professionally and highlights available resources.
  • Exchange Platform: This feature connects caregivers with other relatives and service providers such as doctors, nursing services, and telemedicine providers, fostering a collaborative care environment.
  • Purchasing Avatar: This tool assists caregivers in purchasing necessary items like medicine, resources, and meals. It monitors stock levels and prompts timely re-orders, especially before public holidays.

The app offers a variety of functions to support both the caregiver and the person in need of care. Key functions include:

  1. Daily Routine Module: Assists with documentation and monitoring of nursing progress.
  2. Electronic Patient File: Features an export function and links to family members and general practitioners.
  3. Accelerated Processes: Streamlines application and order processes, as well as time management with cost objects and care providers, independent of suppliers.
  4. Integration of Digital Medical Products: Supports supplier-independent integration of digital medical products and Ambient Assisted Living (AAL) technologies.
  5. Multimedia Transfer: Facilitates the transfer of nursing knowledge through multimedia resources.
  6. Expert Advice: Offers advice from experienced nursing personnel and doctors via video communication, utilizing the patient file.
  7. Development Stages: The system is available in a basic version and will be further developed in stages based on user acceptance.
