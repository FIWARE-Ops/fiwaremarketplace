The Atos Cognitive Data Platform (ACdP) is designed to address the growing need for data aggregation from various sources such as sensors, social networks, open data portals, historical data, and management systems. This platform is essential for developing context-aware applications and delivering smart services to citizens, municipalities, and businesses. By leveraging the NGSI standard, ACdP provides a unique API for accessing context information, which is a widely adopted approach by cities and IT providers globally.

ACdP integrates diverse technologies to offer business intelligence, efficient IoT device management, context data management, advanced data visualization, and robust security. This positions Atos as a leading IT provider for smart city initiatives. The platform also assists cities and customers in analyzing use cases and business scenarios to better meet their needs and enhance services for citizens and users.

Developed with cutting-edge technologies, ACdP offers seamless integration with external systems via API, such as ERPs and issue management systems. It is deployable both in the cloud and on-premise, based on customer requirements, and is customizable and replicable to suit individual needs. The platform utilizes established open-source technologies, reducing adoption and maintenance costs while avoiding vendor lock-in, as it is delivered under an open-source model. Support for open-source components is provided by the FIWARE Foundation, with Atos offering comprehensive support to ensure the platform's technological evolution remains current.

Thanks to its modular architecture, ACdP can be easily extended to adapt to new scenarios or incorporate additional functionalities. The platform is scalable, accommodating varying volumes of sensors, data, and services to deliver the necessary level of service. This scalability ensures that ACdP can grow alongside the needs of its users, making it a versatile solution for smart city challenges.
