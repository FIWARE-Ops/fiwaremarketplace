The eXtended Digital Twin (XDT) solution offers a comprehensive platform for managing and optimizing building operations through its advanced features. It is built on the Digital Enabler platform, which is characterized by its open and scalable cloud infrastructure. This platform integrates various data sources, including sensors, user feedback, and third-party applications, through APIs and connectors. It also supports advanced device management both in the cloud and at the edge.

Key use cases for the XDT include:

  • Energy Efficiency: The platform uses algorithms to enhance energy efficiency within buildings, helping to reduce costs and environmental impact.
  • Well-being and Sustainability: It assesses the level of perceived well-being and sustainability of properties, providing insights for improvements.
  • ESG Management: A dedicated web app is available for energy, HR, and facility managers to manage environmental, social, and governance (ESG) aspects of buildings.
  • Real-time Interaction: Unlike traditional Building Information Modeling (BIM), XDT allows real-time interaction between the physical building and its virtual model, thanks to IoT technologies.
  • Data Collection and Harmonization: The IoT Device Manager collects data from various devices and harmonizes it using the Data Mashup Editor, enabling seamless integration and analysis.
  • Performance Optimization: By simulating scenarios on the virtual model, future performance forecasting and optimization of the building are possible.
  • AI-driven Insights: The solution applies AI algorithms to environmental data to deduce information about indoor comfort and productivity, enhancing decision-making.

Overall, the XDT solution provides a robust framework for improving building operations, enhancing sustainability, and optimizing resource use through its innovative integration of digital twin technology and IoT capabilities.
