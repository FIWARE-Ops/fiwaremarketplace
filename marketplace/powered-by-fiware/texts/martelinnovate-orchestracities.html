Orchestra Cities, developed by Martel Lab, is a cutting-edge smart city data-management platform designed to integrate and manage data from various city verticals. This platform is built on FIWARE and other open-source technologies, providing a centralized solution for cities to manage their data collaboratively. It enables the integration, analysis, and visualization of data from sensors, legacy systems, and citizen inputs, offering a comprehensive suite of functionalities.

Key features of Orchestra Cities include:

  • Flexible dashboard creation with maps and graphs, facilitating easy data sharing.
  • Integrated management of APIs, including Orchestra Cities and third-party APIs, with robust access policies and fault-tolerance.
  • Data management based on open standards, supporting both real-time and historical data.
  • Centralized IoT management, allowing integration of live data from various sensors and devices.
  • Advanced data privacy controls, ensuring data owners maintain control over their data.
  • A visual data ingestion service for designing data pipelines to populate the platform with diverse data sources.

Orchestra Cities is available as a cloud service, ensuring high availability and scalability, with options for private deployments for individual customers. This makes it a versatile tool for smart city managers globally.

The platform is a finalist in the SELECT for Cities competition, collaborating with Antwerp, Copenhagen, and Helsinki to test and validate its capabilities. Use cases include:

  • Reducing traffic congestion in Antwerp through intelligent data-driven decisions.
  • Monitoring pre-diabetes patients in Helsinki with GDPR-compliant smart home data management.

Overall, Orchestra Cities is poised to revolutionize smart city data management, offering a flexible, secure, and collaborative platform for urban innovation.
