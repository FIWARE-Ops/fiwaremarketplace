Snap4City is a versatile, open-source platform widely utilized in smart city and IoT/IoE domains. Developed by the DISIT Lab at the University of Florence, it is an official FIWARE Platform and Solution, ensuring GDPR compliance and having passed PENTest. It is also recognized as an EOSC platform and an official Node-RED Library, among other credentials. Snap4City can be deployed as a service or installed locally without any licensing fees, making it accessible for various applications.

The platform is instrumental in defining city strategies, establishing control rooms, and implementing ethical and explainable AI solutions. It computes key performance indicators crucial for city and industry management. Snap4City integrates the Km4City ontological and semantic model, ensuring data interoperability across diverse IoT devices, models, and physical structures, including FIWARE Smart Data Models. This integration supports decision-makers in cities and industries by providing real-time, open-source solutions for operational actions based on AI predictions and assessments.

Snap4City offers comprehensive insights into contextual trends, early warnings, anomaly detection, and simulation capabilities. These features enable strategic interventions to enhance city services and overall quality of life across various domains, such as Smart Cities and Smart Industry. The platform's flexibility allows for extensions at multiple levels, including data, analytics, tools, applications, MicroServices, and IoT brokers.

Utilizing the FIWARE Orion Broker based on NGSI, Snap4City connects with numerous devices through the IoT Directory and Km4City Knowledge base. It also supports NGSI-compliant IoT Edge solutions and is compatible with other protocols and models like MQTT, AMQP, OneM2M, SigFOX, and LoraWAN. This broad compatibility ensures that Snap4City can adapt to a wide range of IoT environments and requirements, making it a robust solution for smart city and industrial applications.
