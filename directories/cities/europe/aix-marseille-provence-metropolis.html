As the largest urban area in France and comprising 92 municipalities, the Aix- Marseille-Provence Metropolis, located the Mediterranean coast in the South of France is at the forefront of smart cities’ technological innovations. The metropolitan area also topped the ranking of the most advanced smart city in France in 2019, most notably thanks to the number of projects, the number of contracts, the diversity and maturity of those projects.

The city wants to focus its development on improving and operating the city using new technologies based on what has made its reputation for a very long time: its citizens’ quality of life.

Aix-Marseille-Provence puts citizens at heart to bring on an industrial and societal revolution that positively impacts citizens in their daily lives and the modernization of territories.
